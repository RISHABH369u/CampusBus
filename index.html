CampusBus provides campus bus-tracking and related services (live bus locations, ETA, schedules, optional word game/leaderboard). Below: what we collect, why, retention, and your rights.

1. Overview

CampusBus provides campus bus-tracking and related services (live bus locations, ETA, schedules, and an optional word game/leaderboard). This policy explains what information we collect, why, how long we keep it, and your rights.

2. Data we collect
Account & identity
  • Email (from Google Sign-In) and user_id.
  • Optional: full_name and phone only if the user provides them (profile completion).
Location
  • Driver/bus locations (real-time via HiveMQ/MQTT). We only store the last known location and overwrite it with newer updates — no historical archive.
  • Students' device location is not collected unless they opt-in to share it in-app; even then it's not stored persistently unless explicitly requested.
Files / uploads
  • Profile photos or media in feedback/reports (only if uploaded by user).
Diagnostics & logs
  • Device basic info, IP, timestamps and crash logs for debugging and security.
Third-party services

Mapbox (maps/tiles), HiveMQ/MQTT (realtime), and Supabase (auth & DB) process data on our behalf.

3. Why we collect it
  • Provide live bus locations, ETA and routing
  • Store account, leaderboard and game scores
  • Accept feedback/reports (with optional photo)
  • Improve & secure the service (diagnostics)
  • Communicate important notifications
4. Sharing & disclosures
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We share data with Mapbox, HiveMQ, and Supabase only as needed to operate the service.
  • We disclose user data only if legally required (e.g., court order) or to protect safety/security.
5. Retention
  • Bus last-location: only last known location kept; overwritten on each update.
  • Profile & account info: retained while the account exists; removed on account deletion request.
  • Uploaded photos: retained for 90 days (or until user asks for deletion).
  • Logs & diagnostics: retained 30–90 days depending on type.

(Retention values are server-configurable.)

6. Security

We use TLS/HTTPS for all network traffic. Server access is restricted and credentials are rotated periodically. Do not embed secret/service keys in the app — prefer server-side secrets and short-lived credentials.

7. Your choices & rights
  • Revoke/grant location permission via device settings anytime.
  • Request deletion of your account/data by contacting rishcrets@gmail.com.
  • Request a copy of your personal data at rishcrets@gmail.com.
8. Children

The app is intended for college/university students and staff. We do not knowingly collect data from children under the applicable legal age (e.g., under 13). If you believe a child’s data was collected, contact rishcrets@gmail.com.

9. Changes

We will post updates here with a new "Last updated" date. Significant changes will be announced in-app.
