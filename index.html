1. Overview

CampusBus provides a campus bus-tracking and related services (live bus locations, ETA, schedules, and an optional word game/leaderboard). This policy explains what information we collect, why, how long we keep it, and your rights.

2. Data we collect

We collect only what we need to operate the app:

Account & identity
  • Email (from Google Sign-In), user_id (auth ID).
  • Optional: full_name and phone only if the user provides them (e.g., during profile completion). These are used to personalize the app, show leaderboard names, and support account features.
Location
  • Driver / bus locations: we receive real-time GPS coordinates for buses (via HiveMQ/MQTT). We do not keep a historical track — we only store the last known bus location and overwrite it when a newer location arrives.
  • We do not collect students’ device locations unless a student explicitly opts-in to share their location within the app to see their own position on a map; even then student location is not stored persistently unless the user explicitly requests it.
Files / uploads
  • Profile photos or media submitted as part of feedback/reports (only if user uploads). These are stored to support the report and may be retained as described below.
Diagnostics & logs
  • Basic device info, IP, timestamps and crash logs (for debugging). These are used for security and troubleshooting, and are retained for a limited period.
Third-party services

We use Mapbox (maps/tiles), HiveMQ/MQTT (realtime bus locations), and Supabase (auth + DB). These services process data on our behalf.

3. Why we collect it (purposes)

  • Provide live bus locations, ETA and routing.
  • Allow account, leaderboards and game score storage.
  • Accept feedback/reports (with optional photo).
  • Improve and secure the service (diagnostics).
  • Communicate important notifications (e.g., top-1 announcement).

4. Sharing & disclosures

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We share data with the third-party providers above only to the extent needed to operate the service.
  • We do not share user data with external parties except under legal obligation (e.g., court order) or to protect safety/security.

5. Retention (recommended defaults)

  • Bus last-location: only last known location kept; overwritten on each update. No historical location archive.
  • Profile & account info (email, user_id, name, phone): retained while the account exists; removed on account deletion request.
  • Uploaded report photos: retained for 90 days by default (or until user asks for deletion).
  • Logs & diagnostics: retained 30–90 days depending on type.

(Retention durations are configurable server-side and can be adjusted based on privacy/legal requirements.)

6. Security

We use TLS/HTTPS for all network traffic. Access to server data is restricted and credentials are rotated periodically. We recommend not embedding secret/service keys in the app (use server-side secrets, environment variables, and short-lived credentials when possible).

7. Your choices & rights

  • You can revoke or grant location permission at any time via device settings.
  • You can request deletion of your account/data by contacting rishcrets@gmail.com.
  • You can request a copy of your personal data via rishcrets@gmail.com.

8. Children

The app is intended for college/university students and staff. We do not knowingly collect data from children under the applicable legal age (e.g., under 13). If you believe a child’s data was collected, contact rishcrets@gmail.com.

9. Changes to this policy

We will post updates here with a new “Last updated” date. Significant changes will be announced in-app.

If you have privacy questions or want to exercise your data rights, contact: rishcrets@gmail.com
